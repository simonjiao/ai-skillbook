# Review Checklist

Use this checklist before adopting an entry into daily use.

## Evidence

- Is the claim backed by a command, source, run log, screenshot, or code reference?
- Is the environment stated clearly enough to reproduce the result?
- Are date-sensitive claims marked with a checked date?

## Design Fit

- Does the technique solve a recurring problem?
- Does it reduce manual work or ambiguity?
- Does it avoid hiding important decisions behind automation?

## Risk

- Could the workflow leak secrets or private data?
- Could it modify files, repos, issues, or external services unexpectedly?
- Does it have a rollback or disable path?

## Maintenance

- Is ownership clear?
- Are dependencies and prerequisites listed?
- Is there a simple validation command?
